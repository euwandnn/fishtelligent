🔔 What Do the Alerts Mean?
✅
Normal — Fish Are Safe
All water quality parameters are within the safe range. No action needed. The system continues monitoring automatically.
⚠️
Alert — Action Required
One or more parameters are outside the safe range. The system will show you which one — for example, "SALINITY: 25 g/L (safe range: 28–34)". Act immediately to protect the fish.
🤖
AI Alert — Early Warning Detected
The AI (Isolation Forest) has spotted an unusual pattern — even before any reading crosses the threshold. This is an early warning so you can act before the water quality gets worse.
💡 Tip: When you receive an alert — open the Water Quality page to see exactly which parameter has a problem and how far it is from the safe range.
