🔔 Understanding System Alerts
✅
Normal - Fish are Safe
All water quality readings are within the safe range. No action is needed.
⚠️
Warning - Action Needed
One or more water readings are outside the safe range. Check the Water Quality page to see which parameter needs attention.
🤖
AI Alert - Possible Problem Detected
The AI found an unusual water condition, even before it reached the warning level. This gives you time to act early.
💡 Tip: If you receive an alert, open the Water Quality page to see which water parameter needs attention and follow the recommended action.
