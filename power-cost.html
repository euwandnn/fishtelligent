💡 Efficiency Tips
📐 Computation Reference
Step 1 — Wh/day
Watts × Active Hours/day
e.g. Heater: 100W × 12h = 1,200 Wh/day
Step 2 — kWh/mo
Wh/day × 30 ÷ 1,000
e.g. 1,200 × 30 ÷ 1,000 = 36 kWh/month
Step 3 — ₱/month
kWh/month × Rate (₱/kWh)
e.g. 36 × ₱11 = ₱396.00/month
Step 4 — PSU loss
12V load Wh/day × 10%
Switching PSU typical efficiency 85–90%; 10% loss conservative
